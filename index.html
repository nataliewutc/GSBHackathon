Our Mission

At HerHealth, we hope to simulate a safe space for all women where they can reach their potential, stay fit and have fun! By creating programs that cater directly to your goals and personal interests, we make working out that much better. Whether you choose to access our content at home or at the gym, we ensure it’ll keep you motivated which can be the hardest part sometimes. We find progressing alongside other women is greater encouragement as it allows you to feel more comfortable to ask questions and push yourself!

Creators: Amélie Mattheus, Arushi, Natalie Wu and Sharuka Jeyakumar!
